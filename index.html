Listing the 5 best fruits based on deliciousness and nutritional value

Blackberry

Blackberries are not only delicious, but they're also loaded with vitamins, minerals, fiber and antioxidants that boost body functions and improve digestion and blood sugar levels. Antioxidants may reduce inflammation and prevent diseases such as cancer. Blackberries are also low in carbs and calories.

Strawberry

Strawberries, an iconic summer fruit, may be the sweetest way to stay healthy. They are low in sugar and calories but give you a lot of nutritional value that supports your health. Strawberries are loaded in vitamin C and packed with antioxidants, and are known to boost brain power and strengthen your immune system.

Kiwi

Kiwis, known for their fuzzy brown skin, green flesh, and tiny black seeds, are a powerhouse of vitamins that help keep your heart and digestive system healthy. They are sweet and easy to eat and are native to Southwest China. They contain both soluble and insoluble fiber and are exceptionally high in vitamin C.

Banana

Bananas are healthy, delicious, and versatile yellow fruits. Bananas are one of the best fruit sources of vitamin B6, and are respectable sources of vitamin C. The potassium in bananas is also good for your heart health and blood pressure.

Pineapple

Pineapples are a delicious tropical fruit native to South America.

They are a good source of vitamin C, potassium, and fiber.

Hawaii produces about 1/3 of all pineapples in the world and they contain the bromelain enzyme which can break down proteins, so you can use them to tenderize meat.
